PIGS

Pigs, often underestimated in their complexity and significance, hold a multifaceted role in human society that extends far beyond their portrayal as mere livestock. Revered for their intelligence, social behaviors, and adaptability, pigs serve as a compelling subject for exploration into the intricate relationship between humans and animals.
